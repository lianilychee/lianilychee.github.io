BENJI TO THE RESCUE!

Benji the Beaver is the product of six weeks' hard work with a team of three other Olin students and two Babson students. The purpose of Design Nature: Project Transporters was to design and develop an engaging bio-inspired animal-based game for elementary school children, with the final judgement actually passed by local fourth-graders.

Players used a remote controller to navigate Benji around a pool to retrieve logs, which were used to build a dam in order to save Benji's pup. From a user experience perspective, the game was multifaceted and captivating. And from a mechanism design perspective, despite (or perhaps because of - discouragement from the teaching team to pursue a water toy) having a successful Benji was very rewarding.
