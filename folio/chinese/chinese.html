LOST IN TRANSLATION

Coming soon!

I envision a site where I can select a Chinese character and be able to know how to say said character in Mandarin, Cantonese, and Fukien, the three major Chinese dialects. The minimum viable product seems simple enough (probably would involve some pretty robust dictionaries).

Blue-sky goals are where things get exciting. It's one thing to be able to select a character and listen to pronunciations - this assumes literacy, a dangerous assumption given the complexity of the written Chinese language. It's a totally different challenge to input an audio file (user speech input) and have the right character be returned.

Feasible? Not feasible? I'd love to get input!
