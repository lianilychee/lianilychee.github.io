Olin Robotic Sailing

I am the mechanical subteam leader for Olin's Robotic Sailing team. The goal is to develop a boat capable of autonomously traversing the Atlantic Ocean. This boat can then act as a platform for marine research.

s

I developed a new transmission system for the jib, one of the sails. Previously, the jib and the main were powered through a single string and winch system, which, besides not being responsive enough, would frequently tangle. I used a belt-and-pulley system to independently run the jib and the main. Each sail is powered by a AX-108 Dynamixel motor (SPECS OF THE DYNA needed).
