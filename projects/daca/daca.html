STORYTELLING: DACA RECIPIENTS

This is an ongoing project!
Return in Jan 2018 for more details!


In September 2017, my team at Code for Boston began working with the Massachusetts Attorney General's Office, engaging MA constituents to understand undocumented MA residents' experinece with DACA and in the United States.

The project objectives:

  1. Help the AGO understand their clients to litigate more effectively, and
  2. Build public support for the nationwide litigation against repealing DACA.
